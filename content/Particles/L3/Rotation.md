Rotation is a fundamental Protocol Particle of Level 3 allowing for navigation
within any coordinate plane. Rotation requires all Particles of [[Particles/L0/
| Level 0]], [[Particles/L1/ | Level 1]] and [[Particles/L2/ | Level 2]] to be
realized across any given [[Direction.md | dimension]].
