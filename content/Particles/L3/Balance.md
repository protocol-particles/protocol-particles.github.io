Balance is a fundamental Protocol Particle of Level 3 allowing any object to
exist. Balance requires all Particles of [[Particles/L0/ | Level 0]],
[[Particles/L1/ | Level 1]] and [[Particles/L2/ | Level 2]] to be realized
across any given [[Direction.md | dimension]].
