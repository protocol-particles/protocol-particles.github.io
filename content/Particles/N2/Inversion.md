Inversion is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/N2/|Level -2]]
allowing any object to be reflected. Inversion [[Dependence.md|requires]] all
other Particles to be realized across any given [[Direction.md|dimension]].
Inversions may allow new [[Space.md|spaces]] to [[Emergence.md|emerging]] like
negative [[Mathematics.md|numbers]].
