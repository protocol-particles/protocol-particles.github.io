Balance is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P3/|Level 3]] allowing
any object to exist. Balance requires all Particles of [[Particles/P0/ |Level
0]], [[Particles/P1/|Level 1]] and [[Particles/P2/|Level 2]] to be realized
across any given [[Direction.md|dimension]].

### Equilibrium

An equilibrium is one of potentially many states of relative system stability.
[[Phenomena/Strategies/Transition/|Transitions]] between equilibria tend to
follow tipping point dynamics, which means that systems rarely shift linearly
between states of stability. System states of perceived [[Entropy.md|chaos]] may
[[Energy.md|dominate]] any [[Intention.md#Rule-Sets|rule set]] for extended
periods of [[Time.md|time]] as long as the inherent permutations of
multidimensional [[Gravity.md|gravity]] remain imbalanced.
