Balance is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P3/ | Level 3]]
allowing any object to exist. Balance requires all Particles of [[Particles/P0/
| Level 0]], [[Particles/P1/ | Level 1]] and [[Particles/P2/ | Level 2]] to be
realized across any given [[Direction.md | dimension]].
