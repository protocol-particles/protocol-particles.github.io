title: Rotation
alias:
  - Angle,Circle,Turn
---

Rotation is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P3/|Level 3]]
allowing for navigation within any coordinate plane. Rotation requires all
Particles of [[Particles/P0/|Level 0]], [[Particles/P1/|Level 1]] and
[[Particles/P2/|Level 2]] to be realized across any given [[Direction.md|
dimension]].
