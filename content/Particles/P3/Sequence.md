Sequence is a fundamental Protocol Particle of Level 3 enabling causality like
inputs and outputs. Sequence requires all Particles of [[Particles/P0/ | Level
0]], [[Particles/P1/ | Level 1]] and [[Particles/P2/ | Level 2]] to be utilized
across any given [[Direction.md | dimension]].
