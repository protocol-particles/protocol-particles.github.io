Rotation is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P2/|Level +2]]
allowing for navigation within any coordinate plane. Rotation
[[Dependency.md|requires]] all other particles to be realized across any given
[[Direction.md|dimension]]. [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Space/|Spacial]] rotation may
be used for navigational purposes of objects within complex systems.
Dimensional rotation may be part of [[Competition.md#Evolution|evolutionary]]
processes as well as properties of [[Hardness.md|resilience]]. For instance,
electoral systems[^1] may allow for the rotation of leadership structures that
guide complex systems like [[Business.md|businesses]] and [[Governance.md|nation
states]].

[^1]: [Election](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Election)
