Dependence is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P1/|Level +1]]
found in all complex systems. Dependencies [[Emergence.md|emerge]] as
[[Direction.md|dimensional]] relationships between any available form of
[[Interface.md|interfaces]], wherever one set of [[Relativity.md|circumstances]]
may build upon another. Unidirectional[^1] dependencies may be
[[Competition.md|competetive]], if not [[Weaponization.md|harmful]], whereas
mutually beneficial dependencies may allow for forms of
[[Collaboration.md|collaboration]].

[^1]: [Definition Of Unidirectional](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/unidirectional#Adjective)
