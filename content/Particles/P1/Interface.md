Interface is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P1/|Level +1]] found
in all complex systems. Interfaces are specific [[Position.md|points]] of
[[Energy.md|energy]] transfer that allow for the maximization of any given
utility function. Interfaces tell us how the connections between
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/|gravitropic forces]] look like.
