Information is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P1/|Level 1]]
found in all complex systems. Information requires [[Energy.md|energy]] to be
represented via some kind of encoding. Information is generally imperfect since
[[Time.md|time]] does not exist within the fundamental particle barrier.
Observability is therefore a critical concern for players and environments in
game theoretic terminology.
