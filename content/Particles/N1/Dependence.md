Dependencies [[Emergence.md|emerge]] as [[Direction.md|dimensional]]
relationships between any available forms of [[Interface.md|interfaces]] where
one set of circumstances may build upon another. One way dependencies may be
[[Competition.md|competetive]], if not [[Weaponization.md|harmful]]. Mutually
beneficial dependencies may cause forms of [[Symbiosis.md|symbiosis]] to emerge.
