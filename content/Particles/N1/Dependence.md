Dependencies [[Emergence.md|emerge]] as [[Direction.md|dimensional]]
relationships between any available forms of [[Interface.md|interfaces]] where
one set of circumstances may build upon another. Unidirectional[^1] dependencies
may be [[Competition.md|competetive]], if not [[Weaponization.md|harmful]],
whereas mutually beneficial dependencies may allow for forms of
[[Collaboration.md|collaboration]] to emerge.

[^1]: [Definition Of Unidirectional](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/unidirectional#Adjective)
