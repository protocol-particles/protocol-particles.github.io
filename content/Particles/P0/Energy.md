Energy is a fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P0/|Level 0]] found in
all complex systems. Energy is coupled to a quantum state across the
[[Particles/N4|Meta Barrier]] where Energy and [[Emergence.md|Emergence]]
Particles exist in a superposition of simultaneously alternated polarities.

### Power

Power is the higher order ability to bend a system to one's own will, which may
apply to players and environments alike in game theoretic terminology. Power is
never given, power is always taken[^1]. The power to exploit an insufficient
[[Intention.md#Rule-Sets|rule set]] is described by the systemic energy leakage
called [[Weaponization.md#MEV|MEV]].

[^1]: [Where there are humans there are power structures.](https://powerlaw.systems/memo-w08-feb-2025)
