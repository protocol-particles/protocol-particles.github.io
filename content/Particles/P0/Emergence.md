Emergence is the fundamental Protocol Particle of [[Particles/P0/|Level +0]]
found in all complex systems. Emergence is coupled to a quantum state across the
Energetic Barrier [[Particles/N0|N0]] where emergence and [[Energy.md|energy]]
particles exist in a superposition of simultaneously alternated polarities.
