Protocol Particles are the fundamental building blocks of emerging
[[Phenomena/|phenomena]] within [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/|gravitropic
topologies]]. Every additional level of Protocol Particles represents a
conceptual barrier for more complex systems to [[Emergence.md|emerge]]. Note
that it may help to read the periodic table below from left to right.
