Compartmentalization[^1][^2] is a defensive [[Phenomena/Strategies/|strategy]]
to proactively prevent the negative effects of potentially cascading
failures[^3]. In more technical terms, compartmentalization is effectively the
[[Phenomena/Strategies/Separation/|severance]] of
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/index.md#Dimensions|gravitropic dimensions]] across
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/|gravitropic topologies]], so that their
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Gravity/|gravitational]] centers cannot affect one
another anymore. The fundamental principle here is based on the destruction of
the underlying correlating [[Energy.md|forces]], which renders them independent
from one another.

[^2]: [Information Security](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compartmentalization_(information_security)>)
