Weaponization is the kind of interaction that exploits the
[[Intention.md#Rule-Sets|rules]] of a system in [[Inversion.md|unintended]] and
[[Energy.md#Power|forceful]] ways. This [[Emergence.md|process]] typically
unfolds between interest groups of any population, where it resembles key
characteristics of [[Energy.md#MEV|MEV]].
