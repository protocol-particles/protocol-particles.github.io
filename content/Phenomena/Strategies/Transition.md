Transition is the [[Inversion.md|inversion]] of [[Position.md|positions]],
allowing for the evolution[^1] from one [[Information.md|state]] into another.
This [[Emergence.md|process]] is a common property within all complex systems.
Transitions may not be visible, depending on the observer's available
[[Direction.md|dimensions]].

[^1]: [The Art of Learning & Living Life|Josh Waitzkin](https://youtube.com/watch?v=wAnDWfEIwoE)
