Competition is adversarial [[Behaviour.md|behaviour]] that can be thought of as
the [[Inversion.md|inversion]] of [[Symbiosis.md|symbiosis]]. Competition is the
basis of any [[#Evolution|evolutionary]] principle, where the creation and
deletion of traits tries to trend towards an [[Balance.md|equilibrium]] on a
[[Entropy.md#Distributions|distribution]] between life and death.
