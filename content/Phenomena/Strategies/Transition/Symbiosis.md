Symbiosis is the act of [[Balance.md|balanced]] existence between multiple
[[Information.md|states]] or [[Behaviour.md|systems]]. This
[[Emergence.md|process]] is a common[^1] property within all of life. Symbiosis
exploits the [[Inversion.md|inversion]] of needs across populations. Complex
systems may allow for lower order [[Competition.md|inversions of symbiosis]] to
cause higher order forms of symbiosis that are uninverted.

[^1]: [Revolution by Natural Selection - Professor Nick Lane](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lh98fyNtPKM)
