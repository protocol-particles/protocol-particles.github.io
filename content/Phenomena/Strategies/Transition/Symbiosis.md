Symbiosis is the act of [[Balance.md|balanced]] existence between multiple
[[Information.md|states]] or [[Behaviour.md|systems]]. This
[[Emergence.md|process]] is a common[^1] property within all of life. Symbiosis
exploits the [[Inversion.md|inversion]] of needs across populations, which turns
[[#Depenendencies|depenendencies]] into mutually beneficial relationships.
Complex systems may allow for lower order [[Competition.md|inversions of
symbiosis]] to cause higher order forms of symbiosis that are
[[Balance.md#Equilibrium|permanently]] uninverted.

### Dependencies

Dependencies emerge as dimensional relationships between any available forms of
interfaces where one set of circumstances may build upon another. `Dependence`
is a designated [[/#contributions|candidate]] for the Anti Particle status of
the [[Particles/N4|Meta Barrier]] or other.

[^1]: [Revolution by Natural Selection - Professor Nick Lane](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lh98fyNtPKM)
