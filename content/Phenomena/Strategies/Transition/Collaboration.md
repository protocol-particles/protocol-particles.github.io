Collaboration is the act of [[Balance.md|balanced]] existence between multiple
[[Information.md|states]] or [[Behaviour.md|systems]]. This
[[Emergence.md|process]] is a common[^1] property within all of life in the form
of symbiosis[^2]. Collaboration exploits the [[Inversion.md|inversion]] of needs
across populations, which turns [[Dependence.md|depenendencies]] into mutually
beneficial relationships. Complex systems may allow for lower order
[[Competition.md|inversions of collaboration]] to cause higher order forms of
collaboration that are [[Balance.md#Equilibria|permanently]] uninverted.

[^1]: [Revolution by Natural Selection - Professor Nick Lane](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lh98fyNtPKM)

[^2]: [Symbiosis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symbiosis)
