Abstraction generalizes [[Space.md#Context|domain]] specific
[[Information.md|details]] in order to increase the utility function of an
interface. The resulting conceptualization of information allows to be
universally understood by the majority of a population.

### Concepts

Concepts are [[Gravity.md|gravitational]]
[[Entropy.md#Permutations|permutations]] that can only be found in locally
[[Balance.md#Equilibria|convex]] pockets of any given
[[Space.md#Action-Space|action space]].

### Interfaces

Interfaces are specific points of [[Energy.md|energy]] transfer that allow for
the maximization of utility functions. `Interface` is a designated
[[/#contributions|candidate]] for the Anti Particle status of the
[[Particles/N4|Meta Barrier]] or other.
