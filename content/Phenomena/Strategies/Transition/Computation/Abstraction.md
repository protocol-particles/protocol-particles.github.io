Abstraction generalizes [[Space.md#Context|domain]] specific
[[Information.md|details]] in order to increase the utility function of an
[[Interface.md|interface]]. The resulting conceptualization of information
allows to be universally understood by the majority of a population.

### Concepts

Concepts are [[Gravity.md|gravitational]]
[[Entropy.md#Permutations|permutations]] that can only be found in locally
[[Balance.md#Equilibria|convex]] pockets of any given
[[Space.md#Action-Space|action space]].
