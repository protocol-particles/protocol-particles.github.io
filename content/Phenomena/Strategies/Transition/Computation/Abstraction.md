Abstraction generalizes [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Space/index.md#Context|domain]]
specific [[Information.md|details]] in order to increase the utility function of
an [[Interface.md|interface]]. The resulting conceptualization of information
allows to be universally understood by the majority of a population.

### Concepts

Concepts are [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Gravity/|gravitational]]
[[Entropy.md#Permutations|permutations]] that can only be found in locally
[[Balance.md#Equilibria|convex]] pockets of any given [[Action.md|action
space]]. The amount of concepts we know[^1] is estimated to be in the order of
$\frac{1}{10^{600}}$.

[^1]: [the Amount of Concepts we know](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dkpDjd2nHgo&t=2447s)
