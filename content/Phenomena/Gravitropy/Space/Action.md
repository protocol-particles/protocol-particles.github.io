The action space of any given environment describes what is possible within its
own [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Space/|boundary conditions]].
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Entropy/|Entropy]] dictates any
[[Behaviour.md|behaviour]] that can happen will happen eventually, given a
sufficient [[Energy.md|energy]] [[Time.md|time]] relationship. It must be noted
that action spaces are distinct from [[Intention.md#Rule-Sets|rule sets]]
because action spaces are fundamental, whereas rule sets are artificial. What
is not allowed may still be possible.
