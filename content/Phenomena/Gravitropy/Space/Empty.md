---
title: Empty Space
alias:
  - Absence,Nothing,Vacuum
---

Empty space is the [[Inversion.md|inversion]] of occupational space, which plays
a key role in the field of information theory[^1]. Information content may be
increased by the [[Information.md|information]] that is missing or by an action
that has not been done. Information gain via information absence is by no means
paradoxical once we understand that different information particles bind to
different boundary conditions within the surrounding action space. By logical
consequence, the resulting relationships between those boundary conditions can
then either be [[Collaboration.md|collaborative]] or
[[Competition.md|competetive]].

[^1]: [Information Theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_theory)
