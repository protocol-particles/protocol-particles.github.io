Permutations[^1] are combinatorial derivatives of similar inputs. The
distribution of similarity may dictate the accurate classification of what
constitutes an actual permutation of some thing. Structural permutations are the
driving forces of [[Competition.md#Evolution|evolutionary]] processes.

[^1]: [Permutations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permutation)
