Every [[Emergence.md|emerging]] phenomenon unfolds on a probabilistic[^1]
distribution[^2] due to the degree of relativity across its own population.
That means any process may be predictable, if a system's
[[Information.md|inputs]] and [[Behaviour.md|behaviours]] are known in the form
of [[Action.md|action spaces]] and [[Intention.md#Rule-Sets|rule sets]].

[^1]: [Probability](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Probability)

[^2]: [People tend to come up with very different distributions of wrong.](https://powerlaw.systems/memo-w09-mar-2025)
