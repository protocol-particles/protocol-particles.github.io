A permutation[^1] is a combinatorial derivative of similar inputs. The
[[Distribution.md|distribution]] of similarity may dictate the accurate
classification of what constitutes an actual permutation of some thing.
Structural permutations are the driving forces of
[[Competition.md#Evolution|evolutionary]] processes.

[^1]: [Permutation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permutation)
