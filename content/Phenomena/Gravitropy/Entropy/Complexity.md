Complexity[^1] is the property of those systems that are considered too
difficult to [[Energy.md|control]]. Complexity and control are
[[Inversion.md|inversely]] correlated. Increasing and decreasing complexity
within a system does then may cause a decrease or increase in control
respectively. This property of inverse correlation may be deliberately exploited
as most commonly known in [[Direction.md|dimensions]] of
[[Weaponization.md|conflict]].

[^1]: [Complexity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Complexity)
