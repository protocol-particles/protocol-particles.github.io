Hardness[^1] is the desirable form of [[Balance.md#Equilibria|equilibria]] that
exhibit [[Relativity.md|relatively]] low levels of
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Entropy/|entropy]], which is most relevant in
[[Competition.md#Markets|economic]] arenas. Financial assets may be considered
more valuable if they can prove exceptional levels of hardness in key
[[Direction.md|dimensions]] of their existence. Examples for those dimensions
may be in the order of reliable property rights and resistance against
devaluation over long periods of [[Time.md|time]].

[^1]: [Atoms, Institutions, Blockchains](https://stark.mirror.xyz/n2UpRqwdf7yjuiPKVICPpGoUNeDhlWxGqjulrlpyYi0)
