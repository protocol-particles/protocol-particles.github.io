Relativity is an [[Energy.md|energetic]] phenomenon, which we see most clearly
in the [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Gravity/|gravitational]] effects that energy has
on [[Time.md|time]]. More compressed energy slows down time, but only[^1] within
the [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Space/index.md#Proximity|proximity]] of those denser
energy levels. That means the [[Information.md#Subjectivity|subjective]] effects
of a [[Direction.md|dimension]] like time do only apply to the specific
gravitational [[Position.md|position]] itself.

[^1]: [General Relativity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_relativity)
