Symbiosis is the act of [[Balance.md | balanced]] existence between multiple
[[Information.md | states]] or [[Behaviour.md | systems]]. This [[Emergence.md |
process]] is a common property within all of life. Symbiosis exploits the
[[Inversion.md | inversion]] of needs across populations.
