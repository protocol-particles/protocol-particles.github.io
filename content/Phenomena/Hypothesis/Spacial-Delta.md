---
title: Spacial Delta
---

Spacial Deltas refer to the [[Empty.md|lack]] of overlapping areas that allow
for the [[Exploitation.md|exploitation]] of the underlying system. By
definition, the gaps between [[Action.md|action spaces]] and
[[Intention.md#Rule-Sets|rule sets]] enable [[Energy.md#MEV|MEV]]. The inverse
is then also true. If the delta between action spaces and rule sets could
conclusively [[Phenomena/Strategies/Transition/Computation/|proven]] to be
exactly zero, then this very system would be entirely free of MEV. Practically,
no such system is known to exist.
