---
title: Dimensional Asymmetries
---

Dimensional Asymmetries refer to the [[Inversion.md|inverted]] correlations that
the same kind of [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/index.md#Dimensions|gravitropic
dimensions]] have across [[Phenomena/Gravitropy/|gravitropic topologies]]. By
definition, the dimensional correlation within gravitropic topologies is
inverted across gravitropic topologies. The inversion within as compared to
across creates an asymmetry between the same kind of gravitropic dimensions. An
example is formulated in more detail for the [[Sport.md|sports]] industry.
