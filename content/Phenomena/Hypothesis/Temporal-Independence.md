---
title: Temporal Independence
---

Temporal Independence is based on the idea that the [[Direction.md|dimension]]
of [[Time.md|time]] is only of [[Relativity.md|relative]] importance to other
dimensions, most notably here the various dimensions of
[[Phenomena/Gravitropy/Space/|space]]. Space itself may exist in different
[[Permutation.md|shapes]], which [[Competition.md#Evolution|evolve]] on a purely
[[Sequence.md#Causality|causal]] basis without being
[[Information.md#Subjectivity|subjected]] to the linear
[[Sequence.md|schedules]] of time.
